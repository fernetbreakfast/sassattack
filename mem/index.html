SASSMORY

A Simon-style photo memory duel. Pick a pic, watch the sass flash it back, then tap it in order. Miss once and the demon wins.

🔒 Your photo stays on your device — never uploaded.

No photo? Hit START anyway and we'll deal you a sassy default tileset.

HOW TO PLAY

THE LOOP

Each round the demon flashes a sequence of tiles — lighting up a slice of your photo with a tone. Tap them back in the exact same order.

Survive and the sequence grows by one each round. Every round you clear, one more tile of the picture stays revealed — finish the puzzle by going deep.

SCORING

Your score is the highest sequence length you reproduce. One wrong tap and it's over (easy gives you a single retry).

PRIVACY

Your photo is sliced up entirely on your phone with canvas. Nothing is uploaded — only your score and initials ever leave the device.

LEADERBOARD

Top 10 Sassmory runs across all devices. Sign your run when you die to claim a spot — your entry glows gold.
